# 🌐 Facial Recognition Simulator

Inspired by the FC1000 facial recognition device, I developed a web-based simulator that allows users to view the product in 3D and test all its real-world settings, thus improving the experience for customers interested in purchasing the product or for internal company training on how to use it, avoiding expenses related to technical visits.

---

## 📖 About the Project

This project was designed to **streamline training and improve sales processes in the engineering sector** through a simple, efficient, and scalable solution.
It allows users to:

- ✅ Enhance the experience for customers interested in purchasing the product  
- ✅ Enable hands-on training for internal teams  
- ✅ Reduce costs associated with technical visits
